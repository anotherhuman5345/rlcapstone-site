What am I looking at?

The line is the stock's price over the 21 trading days before the chosen date. From clues at that point — recent gains and losses, how bumpy it's been, volume, and the week's news mood — the AI guesses the coming week's risk level. "What actually happened" is the real result, so you can see where it's right and where it's not.
