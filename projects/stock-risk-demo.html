Stock risk classifier — live demo

Pick a real stock-and-week example below. The model predicts whether the next week was Low, Medium or High volatility — in your browser, from that moment's price and sentiment features.

Educational demo — not financial advice.
  • This predicts volatility (how much a stock moves), not direction, and not what you should do. It is a demonstration of machine learning, nothing to trade on.
  • The examples are historical; markets change and models fail. Nobody involved is a licensed financial adviser.

The interesting part of this project isn't the accuracy — it's that adding news sentiment didn't help. See the write-up for why that's the honest, and most useful, result.

What am I looking at?

The line is the stock's closing price over the 21 trading days leading up to the chosen date. From features computed at that point — trailing returns, rolling volatility, volume, and the week's news sentiment — the model predicts the coming week's volatility bucket. "What actually happened" is the real outcome, so you can see where it's right and where it isn't.
