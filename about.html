About this site

Hi — I'm a high school student who's really into AI, and this is where I keep the projects I've built. For each one I did the whole thing myself: found the data, trained the AI on my own computer, tested it honestly, and turned it into an app or demo you can actually try.

I tried to write these up the way I wish tutorials had explained things to me — including the annoying parts most guides skip. Stuff that only works on certain computers, tiny version mismatches that quietly break everything, why I picked the settings I did, and results that are decent but definitely not magic.

How I build each one

  • Training — I train the models with PyTorch on my own graphics card, using free public datasets.
  • Running them — wherever I can, the AI runs right on your device (phone or browser), so the demos work offline and nothing you do gets uploaded.
  • Apps — Flutter for the phone apps, plain web pages for the demos.
  • Being honest — every project shows its real scores on data it never trained on, and admits what it can't do.
