Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

This privacy policy covers the educational RLcapstone applications — MoleCheck, ECG Check, EEG Explorer, Cell Explorer, and Risk Explorer — and the demonstrations on this website. In plain terms: these apps run entirely on your device and collect nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. The apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.

How your data is handled

  • On-device only. Any photo, image, signal, or input you provide is processed locally on your device by an AI model bundled inside the app.
  • Never uploaded. Your inputs are not sent to any server — ours or anyone else's. The apps do not require an internet connection to run inference, and the developer has no server that could receive your data.
  • Not retained by us. Because nothing leaves your device, we never receive, see, or store any of it. Anything the app holds exists only transiently in memory while it runs and is not persisted or shared.

Permissions

Where an app offers camera or photo-library access (for example, MoleCheck), that access is used solely to let you provide an image for on-device analysis. The image is used for that single purpose and is not copied, uploaded, or shared.

Children

These are educational tools intended for a general adult audience and are not directed at children. They collect no data from anyone, including children.

Not a medical or financial service

These applications are educational demonstrations of machine learning. They are not medical devices, are not FDA-cleared or otherwise certified, and do not provide medical, diagnostic, or financial advice. See the full disclaimer.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via the project's source repository on GitHub.
